<div class="vm-intro">
  <h1>🤪 恶搞单词</h1>
  <p class="vm-tagline">雅思词汇那么多，死记硬背太痛苦。<br>六种恶搞记忆法轮番上阵，笑着笑着就记住了。</p>
  <div class="vm-methods-grid">
    <div class="vm-method-box">
      <div class="vm-m-title"><span class="vm-m-emoji">🎤</span> 谐音记忆<span class="vm-m-label vm-tag-yellow">核心</span></div>
      <div class="vm-m-desc">每个单词配中文谐音，再编一句荒诞段子。大脑对有画面的信息记得更牢——越离谱，越难忘。所有单词必带谐音。</div>
      <div class="vm-m-example vm-ex-yellow">🎤 abandon — 「阿板凳」→ 第一个放弃的人，去坐了冷板凳 🪑</div>
    </div>
    <div class="vm-method-box">
      <div class="vm-m-title"><span class="vm-m-emoji">🔧</span> 拆词记忆<span class="vm-m-label vm-tag-blue">辅助</span></div>
      <div class="vm-m-desc">把长单词拆成词根词缀，像拼乐高一样理解单词的构造逻辑，举一反三。</div>
      <div class="vm-m-example vm-ex-blue">🔧 unprecedented — un(无)+pre(之前)+cedent(发生) → 史无前例</div>
    </div>
    <div class="vm-method-box">
      <div class="vm-m-title"><span class="vm-m-emoji">😂</span> 段子记忆<span class="vm-m-label vm-tag-pink">辅助</span></div>
      <div class="vm-m-desc">围绕单词编一个极短小故事，用情境包裹词义。故事越荒诞越容易回忆。</div>
      <div class="vm-m-example vm-ex-pink">😂 ambiguous — 面试官说"你表现很 ambiguous"，你猜了半天也不知道过没过 🤷</div>
    </div>
    <div class="vm-method-box">
      <div class="vm-m-title"><span class="vm-m-emoji">💡</span> 联想记忆<span class="vm-m-label vm-tag-green">辅助</span></div>
      <div class="vm-m-desc">将单词与画面、场景或已知词汇建立关联。具象化的想象让抽象单词瞬间立体。</div>
      <div class="vm-m-example vm-ex-green">💡 thrive — three + live，活出三倍生命力，想象一棵树疯狂生长 🌱</div>
    </div>
    <div class="vm-method-box">
      <div class="vm-m-title"><span class="vm-m-emoji">👯</span> 形近记忆<span class="vm-m-label vm-tag-purple">辅助</span></div>
      <div class="vm-m-desc">把长得像的词拉出来对比，一次记住一窝。防止考试时"好像见过但不确定"。</div>
      <div class="vm-m-example vm-ex-purple">👯 exceed — exceed(超过) vs excess(过量) vs excel(擅长)，三胞胎分清！</div>
    </div>
    <div class="vm-method-box">
      <div class="vm-m-title"><span class="vm-m-emoji">🎵</span> 口诀记忆<span class="vm-m-label vm-tag-orange">辅助</span></div>
      <div class="vm-m-desc">把单词编成顺口溜或打油诗，节奏感帮你锁住记忆，念一遍就上口。</div>
      <div class="vm-m-example vm-ex-orange">🎵 leisure — 「累了就 leisure，休闲时刻别忙碌」🎶</div>
    </div>
  </div>
  <p class="vm-hint">👈 从左侧导航栏选择单词本开始学习</p>
</div>
