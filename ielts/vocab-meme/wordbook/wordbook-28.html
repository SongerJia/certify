The distribution of food aid to remote villages was hampered by damaged roads.

district/ˈdɪstrɪkt/

The historic district of the city attracts millions of tourists each year.
#2782

Please do not disturb the patients during their afternoon rest period.

The police were called to deal with a disturbance at the late-night party.

The car skidded off the icy road and ended up in a muddy ditch.

The submarine began to dive deeper into the ocean to avoid detection.

The experienced diver explored the coral reef at a depth of thirty metres.

A traffic diversion was put in place while the main road underwent repairs.

Traffic was diverted away from the city centre due to a major gas leak.

The issue of immigration continues to divide public opinion across the country.

There is a clear division of responsibilities between the two departments.

The divorce rate has been steadily declining in many developed countries.
